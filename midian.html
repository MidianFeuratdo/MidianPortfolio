Blazblue Chronophantasma Nu-13 Frame Data And Overview

    Move List Match-ups Neutral Offensive options Defensive options


After the transition to chronophantasma nu-13 has picked up a lot of new tricks. Primarily her new move phase shift. With it she can access moves from both her Calamity trigger and Chronophantasma predecessors.
With her projectiles and range nu-13 has a great neutral game. She can anti-air with her 2d and 6d at mid-range. With her 5a,6a, and 3c she can anti-air up close. 2b has good range hits low and has great frame advantage.
